Privacy Policy – River Rush

Last updated: May 2, 2026

River Rush is a mobile arcade game developed as an independent project. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Information We Collect

River Rush does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details.

Data Storage

All game data (such as scores) is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

River Rush does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

River Rush is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

Internet Access

River Rush does not require an internet connection and does not make any network requests.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at: gameriverrun@gmail.com
